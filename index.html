About

Trusted, certified, and always on the go

Elizabeth Campos is a New Jersey-commissioned Notary Public with hands-on experience in loan signings, real estate documents, legal paperwork, and a wide range of personal and business notarizations.

As a mobile notary, Elizabeth travels to clients across New Jersey — eliminating the hassle of finding a notary office and fitting appointments into busy schedules. Whether it's a last-minute signing or a planned closing, Elizabeth provides professional, confidential, and efficient service every time.

Elizabeth is also a certified Notary Signing Agent, trained to handle complex loan and mortgage signings with accuracy and care. She is committed to making every experience smooth, stress-free, and professional.
